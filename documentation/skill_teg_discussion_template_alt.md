Instructions 

The Technical Engineering Group (TEG) sessions are conducted in one of two format types: 

Time-boxed brainstorming (up to 30 minutes) session 

Discussion and feedback session 

Select the appropriate category for discussion or brainstorming 

Scheduling a session 

Please refer to the guide me document for the TEG process in the HEAT Sharepoint  

Select a discussion category from the list below 

Upload the one-pager document to the TEG SharePoint 

Create a backlog item and task in the TEG Azure DevOps board 

Add the link to the one-pager document to the backlog item "One Page Link" field 

Ensure that the "Tentative Completion Date" field is populated with the date of the topic Completion Date" field is populated with the date of the topic 

An automated email will be sent on the day of the TEG session with your topic 

For questions or concerns, send an email to the HEAT team  

DL_HS_HRSA_EntArchitecture@reisystems.com

Guidelines for the TEG brainstorming session: 

The purpose of the time-boxed brainstorming session is to provide a forum to 

Flush out a design or process 

Get ideas for design alternatives 

Brainstorm ideas for a design or process 

Identify any gaps in a design or process 

Please ensure that brainstorming topic details are documented in the one-pager. 

The session will be time-boxed, so it is essential to do as much preparation up front to get as much out of the session as possible 

The TEG team will moderate the session to ensure that everyone gets a chance to give their input 

The session will be hosted during the usual TEG time slot on Tuesdays or Thursdays at 2:30 – 4:00 PM
